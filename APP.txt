Scenario: Students find difficulty in finding books
Solution: We'll create an app that provides features like
	a. Students can search for any book
	b. Students can see if their books are available
	c. Students can check the location of the book store
	d. Students can contact for further information (contact details for book store)
	e. Students can check the pricing of the book (from each vendor)
	f. Students can even sell their old books
Screens:
a. Profile Screen
b. Home Screen
c. Details Screen
d. Selling product Info Screen
